Full Job Description
Iveta 545 Inc. is looking for experienced servers for our new contemporary casual restaurant at 545 Pacific Ave. in Santa Cruz.

The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner. Full or part-time shifts are available with a minimum of 20 hours per week, including weekends and holidays.

Expectations

We’re looking for individuals who:

are attentive, friendly, courteous and have a professional demeanor along with great communications and customer service skills;
have a minimum of 2 years’ experience serving in a busy restaurant;
are passionate about hospitality, food and can help create a great dining experience or diffuse a tense situation, if necessary;
can converse easily with guests, multitask, manage several tables at once and respond well under pressure; and
can work quickly and accurately as part of a team with the stamina to be on their feet for 6+ hours.
Duties

greeting guests and engaging in friendly conversation to create a hospitable and welcoming environment;
attending to guests during a meal to help them place orders and making sure food and beverages are delivered in a timely manner;
become knowledgeable about the menu to explain dishes to guests, make menu recommendations and clarify any concerns about allergies or styles of food preparation;
take orders and input them into the POS system; serve food and beverages; refill drinks; check on other tables and assist with completion of guest transactions;
help maintain a clean and sanitary environment by bussing dishes, resetting tables, restocking server stations, doing light prep work to assist line cooks and completing other closing tasks.
